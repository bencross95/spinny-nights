Lynks Afrikka

A performer, an entrepreneur, and now a religion, Lynks Afrikka is the brainchild of Bristol based producer Elliot Brett. Lynks draws upon the legacy of 90s club-kid culture, merging music, drag and theatre in an unapologetically queer and utterly unique way.

The project was born out of Brett’s frustration with the current state of dance music; unsatisfied with the hyper-cool, straight-faced, fun-deprived club-nights now omnipresent, Lynks’ aim was singular — to bring pure unadulterated joy to its audiences.
