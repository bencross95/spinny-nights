about us

Initially founded in Bristol as a platform for a group of students to expose various music projects in immersive environments, the nights have brought cutting-edge, infectious live performances to a myriad of independent and underground venues across the city and beyond. Previous events have hosted the likes of LICE, Yama Warashi, Kelora, E B U, Mercy’s Cartel, Sistertalk, Oro Swimming Hour …
