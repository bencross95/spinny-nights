Spinny Nights first formed to provide a platform upon which a small community of students could showcase their various projects in immersive environments. We have since blossomed, bringing cutting-edge, infectious live performances to independent and underground venues across Bristol and beyond. Previous nights have hosted the likes of LICE, Yama Warashi, Kelora, E B U, Mercy’s Cartel, Park Motive, Sistertalk, Lynks Afrikka, Oro Swimming Hour…
